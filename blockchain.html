The Development of Blockchain Technology in Supply Chain Management

The focus of my project, "The Development of Blockchain Technology in Supply Chain Management," is to investigate how blockchain technology can transform supply chain processes. Blockchain, known for its decentralized and immutable nature, has the potential to address many challenges faced by the supply chain industry, including transparency, traceability, and security. By providing a single source of truth, blockchain can enhance trust among stakeholders, reduce fraud, and streamline operations.

Literature review

My project journey begins with a comprehensive literature review. This review aims to provide a snapshot of the current state of research in the field of blockchain technology as it applies to supply chain management.

White paper

Building upon the insights gained from the literature review, I developed a white paper that delves deeper into the practical applications and implications of blockchain technology in supply chains.

Public document

The third component of my project is to translate academic knowledge into a format accessible to the general public. This public document aims to demystify blockchain technology and its impact on supply chain management for a broader audience.

Professional presentation

In addition to these written components, I presented my findings in an in-person presentation. The experience of presenting my work to a large setting helped refine my understanding and communicate the significance of blockchain technology in supply chain management effectively.
